2024-09-28 PV:

For many years, our research team has been dedicated to exploring the relationship between emotional intelligence and health medicine. With the support of the “Tianjin Key Laboratory of Student Mental Health and Intelligent Evaluation,” jointly established by our School of Computer and Information Engineering and the Faculty of Psychology, as well as the university-level “Interdisciplinary Research Team for Cognitive and Affective Computing,” we have accumulated extensive technical achievements in interdisciplinary research on intelligent medicine. The principal investigator has led several projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China Youth Program, sub-projects of the National Key R&D Program, and the Joint Fund of Equipment Pre-research and the Ministry of Education. Team members have also led five projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, including general and youth programs, and have participated in over ten provincial and ministerial-level projects, such as key projects under the Tianjin Applied Basic Research Program, Tianjin Support Program, and various international cooperation projects. Through these research experiences, we have gained an in-depth understanding of core technical details and development processes in the field. We have established a solid technical foundation in brain imaging data acquisition, MRI and EEG signal processing and analysis, emotion detection, automated depression severity analysis, deep learning techniques, and software design and development, providing essential technical support for the successful advancement of this project.
